Markdown 使用base64 编码嵌入图片

加入markdown代码段

![](data:image/png;base64,xxx)

xxx为生成的base64编码

python转换脚本

from base64 import b64encode

fig_path = 'path/xxx.jpg'

with open(fig_path, 'rb') as f:
    tmp_read = f.read()

ret = b64encode(tmp_read)
print(ret.decode('utf-8'))
